How It Works

1

You upload your resume

We extract 60–120 skills across 6 categories: technical, tools, methodologies, leadership, industry knowledge, and soft skills.

2

Every 6 hours, we pull jobs

Hundreds of real openings matched to your skill profile, pulled from across industries and locations you care about.

3

Skill-by-skill comparison

Each job's requirements are parsed and compared against yours. We identify matches, gaps, and the closest transferable skills.

4

Email alerts with AI analysis

Matches above your threshold are emailed to you with a detailed breakdown — skills matched, gaps identified, and an overall recommendation.

5

Jobs you'd never find on your own

The result: roles you're qualified for that you'd never discover on a traditional job board. Cross-industry matches that leverage what you already know.
